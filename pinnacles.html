About

Pinnacles National Park is a park located near the Central Valley in California.

It is accessible from the east via CA-25, and from the west via CA-146. The west and east entrances are only connected with hiking trails and with highways outside the park.

The pinnacles that Pinnacles National Park is named for originated years ago in the Mojave Desert and got to Pinnacles along the San Andreas Fault.

For more information, see the Pinnacles website.
